What the field already shows

Three things worth knowing before you start.

DEMAND

A market moving faster than the evidence.

The global peptide market was worth about 52.6 billion dollars in 2025, and GLP-1 medicines alone made up roughly 14 percent of all prescription drug spending in the United States.

REGULATION

The rules are being rewritten right now.

The Food and Drug Administration compounding advisory committee meets in July 2026 to decide the status of seven peptides, including BPC-157 and TB-500.

WHAT PEOPLE REPORT

Recovery and appetite, with a quality problem.

Recovery, appetite control, and energy are the reasons people most often start. They buy online and have concerns about quality control; Google says peptide is one of the fastest-growing search terms, so demand is outpacing what people know, and they're reporting everything from adverse events to major gains without any detail.
